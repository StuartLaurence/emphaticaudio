About Emphatic Audio

Emphatic Audio is a home-based studio in Austin, Texas, built around real instruments, real amps, and a workflow that understands what it’s like to be in a band. No pressure, just good sounds and honest feedback.

  • Comfortable, relaxed space: track without the “big studio” anxiety tax.
  • Engineer/producer who plays in working bands and understands loud guitars, fast drums, and vocals that actually cut.
  • Flexible scheduling for real lives: day jobs, kids, gigs, and weird work hours welcome.
  • Bring your own ideas, or get help with arrangements, tones, and performance.

Who is this for?

Emphatic Audio is ideal for local bands who want a serious recording without getting lost in a giant commercial facility.

Great fit for:
• Punk / metal / hard rock bands
• DIY artists ready for a step up in sound
• Bands who want to record live in a room and keep the energy

About Me

I’m a guitar player, studio rat, and long-time member of the Austin heavy music scene. If you’ve spent time in local clubs, there’s a decent chance we’ve crossed paths.

Bands & background

I’ve spent decades playing, writing, and recording in loud bands — from classic heavy metal to punk, doom, and weird rock. I’m not just hitting record; I understand what it takes to actually play this stuff.

That experience feeds directly into how I track and mix: helping with tones, performance, and keeping sessions focused while still fun.

Video & content

I also do video editing and basic production, especially for music-related projects: performance clips, live session videos, and short social-media-ready pieces that help bands get seen.

If you’re interested in adding simple video to your recording session — even just a few good performance angles — mention it when you reach out and we can talk options.

Pricing

Simple, band-friendly rates. Pick a project-style option or pay straight time. No surprises — you’ll know exactly what you’re spending.

Tracking

$200 / day

Recording sessions for full-band basics, overdubs, and getting solid takes without feeling rushed.

  • MP3 rough mixes included
  • Pay at end of each session day
  • Dropbox links for roughs

A “day” typically runs up to ~6 hours of tracking time.

Final Mix

$60 / song

Final mixes I start on my own, off session. Later we'll work together on tweeks and revisions.

  • Final stereo WAV + MP3
  • Delivered via Dropbox link
  • Reasonable revisions included

Major re-tracking or big arrangement changes may be billed hourly.

Hourly Option

$30 / hour

Prefer to pay straight time? No problem — we punch in/out and you pay for exactly what we do.

  • Punch-in / punch-out timer (Clockify)
  • Pay at end of each session day
  • Great for one-offs or unknown scope

Please tell us you want hourly before we start.
